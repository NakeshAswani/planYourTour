How to reach Manali?

We will manage it for you but you can get a custom Manali couple package and we will pick you up from Manali if you ask for that. Here is how you can reach Manali—

By road:

Manali and Kullu are connected to almost every big northern city of India by the bus network. You can travel via bus from cities like New Delhi, Shimla, Kullu, Leh, Rishikesh, Chandigarh, etc.

By Train:

The Joginder Nagar Railway Station to the nearest railway station to Manali and is well connected to various railway stations across India.

By Air:

The nearest airports to Shimla are Indira Gandhi International Airport and Chandigarh International Airport. You can book a cab or bus from there to Manali.
