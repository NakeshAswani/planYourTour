Overview

If you want to experience the breeze of the Himalayas and witness the beauty of glaciers in India, Shimla and Manali are your go-to destinations. From spiritual places like Hadimba Temple and Vashisht Temple to places with adventure activities, Manali has so much to offer. You will also witness the mesmerizing beauty of the Queen of Hills, Shimla. It has a wingspan of seven hills in the Himalayas. Enjoy Jakhoo hill and worship Lord Hanuman there. Ridge road and Himalayan zoo are yet other astonishing places that you can not afford to miss in Shimla. Kullu and Manikaran also have some amazing spots to visit like hot water springs and the famous Gurudwara. The meals and street food of the Himachal also play out to be an ultimate partner in the mountains. The nature in this part of India is a treat to the eyes as the snow-headed mountains are nothing but beautiful.

About Shimla Manali Tour Package From Mumbai :

Visit the cities with the most natural vibe in the northern part of India with a trip to Himachal. Shimla, Kullu, and Manali have mythology attached to it and you will visit religious places in these cities. There are plenty of adventure activities in Manali and Shimla that will increase the excitement of the trip. Visit snow-capped mountains, forests, and the Beas river to make your trip a great one. The weather is also completely different from Mumbai and you will love being in the city as it has soul-soothing climate conditions. Get to know about the Pahadi lifestyle and make memories in Himachal.

How to reach Manali?

By road:

Shimla is connected to almost every northern part of India by the bus network. You can travel via bus from New Delhi, Shimla, Kullu, Leh, Rishikesh, Chandigarh, etc.

By Train:

The railway network is also well-connected to various cities across India.

By Air:

The nearest airports are Indira Gandhi International Airport and Chandigarh International Airport. You can book a cab or bus from there to Shimla or Manali. You can also reach Shimla with the help of a personal vehicle.
