Overview

A trip to Andaman and Nicobar islands is considered the best way to escape from your busy life. The boasts of beaches, clear waters, and thriving marine life are waiting for you to visit. The capital city of Port Blair is the gateway to these islands, where you can soak in the rich history and culture of the region.

Key information about the Andaman Nicobar tour package:

Starting point: Port Blair
Endpoint: Port Blair
Duration of trip: 6 Days 5 Nights

  • Buffet dinner on all days
  • Sightseeing as mentioned in the itinerary
  • Experienced travel guide
  • Experienced driver and transportation
  • All types of applicable taxes on traveling
