Overview

Make the most of the trip by taking a lengthy journey and exploring every inch of Andaman and Nicobar. Visit the best islands in the world and spend some time exploring marine life. Get a tribal life view and relive ancient history with the Andaman Nicobar tour package.

Key information about the Andaman Nicobar tour package:

Starting point: Port Blair
Endpoint: Port Blair
Duration of trip: 7 Days 6 Nights

Tour Package Inclusions:

  • Accommodation in a hotel as per the itinerary
  • breakfast on all days
  • Sightseeing as mentioned in the itinerary
  • Experienced travel guide
  • Experienced driver and transportation
  • All types of applicable taxes on traveling

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Port Blair and a trip to Cellular Jail
  • Arrive in Port Blair for pick-up and hotel check-in
  • Visit Corbyn Cove beach
  • Water sports activities at the beach
  • Visit to Cellular Jail
  • Late evening light and sound show at historical Cellular Jail
  • Overnight stay at hotel
Day 2: Ross Island and Coral Island visit
  • A ferry ride from Port Blair to Ross Island
  • Visit North Bay Island, also known as Coral Island
  • Enjoy water activities like scuba diving and glass-bottom boat rides
  • Overnight stay at hotel
Day 3: Visit Havelock Island
  • The morning ferry from Port Blair to Havelock Island
  • Hotel check-in at Havelock Island and rest
  • Visit Radhanagar Beach
  • Overnight stay at hotel
Day 4: Explore Havelock Island and Elephant Beach
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Boat ride to the Elephant Beach
  • Enjoy water sports and activities
  • Travel back to Havelock Island
  • Explore local market
  • Overnight stay at hotel
Day 5: Visit Neil Island
  • Travel to Neil Island from Havelock Island
  • Hotel check-in
  • Visit Bharatpur, Sitapur, and Laxmanpur Beach
  • Enjoy the sunset view and explore the local market
  • Overnight stay at hotel
Day 6: Explore Baratang Island
  • A ferry ride from Neil Island to Baratang Island
  • Visit the famous limestone caves
  • Explore the nearby mangrove forests
  • Return to Port Blair
Day 7: Departure
  • Fresh n up from hotel
  • Check out from your hotel and depart with memories of a lifetime
